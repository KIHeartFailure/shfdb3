For each post in Swede-HF, a set of controls was chosen in a ratio of 5:1. Since a person can have more than one registred post in Swede-HF a case can have more than 5 controls in total spread over the different posts.

Eligible controls were persons without re-used and changed Swedish personal identity numbers (PINs) and free of HF, defined either in the National Patient registry (ICD-10: I110 I130 I132 I255 I420 I423 1425 I426 I427 I428 I429 I43 I50 J81 K761, ICD-9: 414W 425E 425F 425G 425H 425W 425X 428) or Swede-HF, at the end of the year of the index date for the case and matched by gender, year of birth and county of residence at the index. For posts with age â‰¥ 90 years, controls were only matched by gender and year of birth due to the smaller available number of controls.

The selection of controls was performed wihout replacment.

A control was allowed to become a case after the year of the index date for the case.
